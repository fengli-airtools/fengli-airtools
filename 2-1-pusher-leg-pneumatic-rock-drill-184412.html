Pneumatic Rock Drill

TY24

The pneumatic rock drill TY24 adopts a novel design to simplify operation, including air-water linkage system, quick leg retraction mechanism, air pressure adjustment system, etc. Moreover, a silencer is applied to greatly reduce the noise level, thus creating a better working environment for operators.

In general, compared with similar products, this pusher leg mounted rock drill (or simply pusher leg rock drill) is characterized by light weight, low noise and high efficiency.

Application
Our jackleg drill is widely used for mining, tunnel driving and other rock drilling jobs, and it is an indispensable pneumatic tool for metallurgy, coal, railway, transportation, capital construction and national defense industries where rock excavation is needed.
