Home » About Us » Development History

2004
FENGLI was set up in this year. At the beginning, there were about 10 employees, and the factory area was only 3500 square meters.

2005
We independently developed a series of pneumatic picks including B87C, B67C and so on. These pick hammers were extensively utilized in mining, road construction, bridge construction and other infrastructure construction projects.

2006
After two years of effort, our air tools were highly praised by global clients, leading to a pleasant order boom �C fast increase in orders. Given this, we decided to expand our production capacity by introducing several CNC lathes. At that time, the total number of employees reached up to 30.

2007
FENGLI gained the import and export trading right, and started to explore overseas market. In the same year, we successfully developed TPB-90, TPB-60, TPB-40 and TCA-7 pneumatic breakers that were comparable with TOKU products.

2008
We delivered 200 sets of B87C pneumatic breaker and corresponding accessories to an American client for the first time. Due to superior product quality and sincere service, this client has maintained cooperative relationship with us since then.

2010
To better meet the evergrowing market demand, we moved to a new production base which was 14000 square meter large. After relocation, we had over 60 employees in total, while some leading-edge forging equipment and heat treatment equipment were introduced.

2011
Our company took part in the Hardware + Tools Middle East 2011 so as to promote our pneumatic picks. During the exhibition, our pick hammers had drawn the attention of many customers.

2012
FENGLI passed the ISO 9001:2008 Quality Management System Certification, and we also attended the Bauma China 2012.

2013
Our pneumatic breakers and rock drills were more and more popular on the market, adding up to an annual sales volume of more than 30 million CNY.
