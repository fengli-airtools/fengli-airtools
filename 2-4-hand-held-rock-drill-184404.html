Hand Held Rock Drill

Y26

The hand held rock drill Y26 is primarily developed for drilling, secondary blasting and similar jobs in mines, road construction sites and water conservancy projects. It fits for both dry and wet drilling, and it could create vertical or inclined blast holes in medium-hard and hard rocks.

Technical Data of Hand Held Rock Drill Y26

Weight 26kg
Dimensions 650×534×125mm
Cylinder diameter 65mm
Piston stroke 70mm
Impact energy ≥48J at 0.5Mpa
≥30J at 0.4Mpa
Drilling frequency ≥27Hz at 0.5Mpa
≥23Hz at 0.4Mpa
Air consumption ≤50L/s at 0.5Mpa
≤47L/s at 0.4Mpa
Torque ≥14N·m at 0.5Mpa
≥11N·m at 0.4Mpa
Water pressure 0.3Mpa
Air hose inner diameter 19mm
Water hose inner diameter 13mm
Drilling diameter 34-42mm
Max. drilling depth 5m
Working temperature -30°C~+50°C
Shank size H 22×108mm or H 25×108mm
