Pneumatic Breaker and Pneumatic Rock Drill Manufacturer

As a professional air tools manufacturer and wholesaler in China, FENGLI offers pneumatic breaker, rock drill and pneumatic drill rod in a wide range of models. Our pneumatic pick breakers have been field proven to be very effective for breaking ice, brick, frozen soil, reinforced concrete, and other hard objects, while our pneumatic drills could create several blast holes in rocks to perform rock blasting. With over a decade of experience in pneumatic tools industry, FENGLI has successfully cooperated with TOKU, SULLAIR and Gardner Denver.
