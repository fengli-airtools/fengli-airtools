Pneumatic Breaker

TCA-7

The pneumatic breaker TCA-7 is a rugged and lightweight air tool intended for light to medium construction work and plant maintenance, such as the demolition of floor, pavement, asphalt, bridge deck, frozen ground, masonry wall, etc.

Feature
Our pneumatic breaker exhibits superior durability since it is constructed of alloy steel forgings, and it uses an integral oiler to realize continuous lubrication. In addition, this pick hammer works well even under harsh conditions owing to the implementation of a four-bolt backhead.

Technical Data of Pneumatic Breaker TCA-7

Piston diameter 35mm
Piston stroke 120mm
Impact rate 1250 blows/min
Air consumption 1.0m3/min (35.3cfm)
ID of air hose 7.2mm
Shank size 26×80mm
Air inlet size 3/4PT
Total length 465mm
Net weight 7.2kg (15.8lb)
