FENGLI is a dedicated pneumatic tool manufacturer and supplier in China, and we mainly provide worldwide customers with various models of pneumatic breaker, rock drill and pneumatic drill rod. These high-quality yet affordable air tools offer ideal solutions for pavement breaking, rock drilling and other applications.

Inaugurated in 2004 and with a floor space of 14,000 square meters, our company is only 50km from Beijing, and this brings us extra convenience when shipping. Since our foundation, we have been focused on the R&D of new air powered tools, and we have also cooperated with TOKU, Gardner Denver, SULLAIR and other world-class enterprises to share technical innovation. Along with our state-of-the-art production equipment and well-experienced technical staff, our pneumatic breaker and rock drill are highly appreciated by even the most captious clients in Europe and America. For example, our pneumatic breakers, including TPB-50, TPB-60, TPB-90, B87C, B47 and SK10, are considered to be of leading quality around the globe!

Now, we have gained ISO 9001:2008 Quality Management System Certificate, and we are also honored as Quality and Credit Guaranteeing Enterprise by the China Light Industry Products Quality Guarantee Centre.

FENGLI tries to manufacture high-quality pneumatic breakers and rock drills to satisfy customers�� expectations, and we are looking forward to cooperating with you!
