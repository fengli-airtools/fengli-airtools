Pneumatic Breaker

SK10
Spare Parts

The pneumatic breaker SK10 is manufactured using SULLAIR technologies, and it is fitted with a tubular diverter valve so as to distribute compressed air at the top and bottom of the piston alternatively in a cycle, thus driving the piston to move up and down. As a result, the shank is driven into rocks or ore beds to break them into pieces.

Application
1. In mining industry, this pneumatic breaker is able to break up soft rocks and cut off coal beds to facilitate mining operation.
2. As for civil engineering (municipal construction, underground construction, etc.), our pneumatic pick offers a perfect solution for breaking ice, concrete, frozen soil, damaged pavement, etc.
3. In industrial enterprises, our pick hammer could be used for the demolition of concrete foundation which was intended for installing old equipment, and it also fits for places where impact force is required, like mounting/dismounting track pins of tractors or tanks.

Technical Data of Pneumatic Breaker SK10

Total length 498mm
Total weight 10kg
Cylinder diameter 38mm
Piston stroke 155mm
Piston weight 0.9kg
Working pressure 0.63Mpa
Impact energy ≥39.3J
Impact rate ≥18.4Hz
Air consumption ≤20L/s
ID of air hose 16mm
