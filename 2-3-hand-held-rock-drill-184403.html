Hand Held Rock Drill

Y24

The hand held rock drill Y24 is a light but robust jackhammer for dry drilling of rocks, and it is developed to perform light to medium construction and plant maintenance work, like the demolition of floor, pavement, bridge, asphalt, frozen ground, masonry wall, etc.

Feature
This pneumatic rock drill is manufactured from alloy steel forgings to enhance its durability, and its four-bolt back head enables it to work normally even under extreme conditions. In addition, the FY200B lubricator/oiler provides continuous lubrication.


Technical Data of Hand Held Rock Drill Y24

Weight 24kg
Air consumption ≤50L/s
Drilling diameter 34-42mm
Max. drilling depth 5m
Shank size 22×108mm
Working pressure 0.4Mpa
